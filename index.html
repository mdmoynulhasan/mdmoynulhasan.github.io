Md Moynul Hasan

Email: mdmoynul.hasan@rockets.utoledo.edu

I am a second year M.S. student in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Toledo, advised by Prof. Anju Gupta. My research focuses on pool boiling heat transfer, with an emphasis on experimental investigations. I am also exploring Scientific Machine Learning (SciML) and am currently taking the AI in Science and Engineering course by ETH Zürich to expand my expertise.

Previously, I earned my B.S. in Naval Architecture and Marine Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET), where I worked on aerodynamic modeling of airfoils using Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs) under Prof. N. M. Golam Zakaria and contributed to biomedical image processing and deep learning research with Prof. Taufiq Hasan and Prof. Mohammed Imamul Hassan Bhuiyan. I enjoy both experimental and computational research. My goal is to integrate machine learning with physics based models to enhance predictive modeling in engineering applications.
