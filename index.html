I am a first-year MS student in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Toledo. Previously, I completed my undergraduate studies in Naval Architecture and Marine Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), and worked as a Research Assistant at the same department. During my undergraduate thesis, I worked under the supervision of Prof. N. M. Golam Zakaria and used Artificial Neural Networks to predict the aerodynamic characteristics of airfoils. Additionally, I worked on a research project related to Biomedical Image Processing & Deep Learning, under the guidance of Assoc Prof. Taufiq Hasan and Prof. Mohammed Imamul Hassan Bhuiyan. My interests is in Data-driven Engineering.
